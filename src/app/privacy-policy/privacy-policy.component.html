1. Introduction

Welcome to WebPizza RAG POC ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our 100% client-side AI document chat application.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

WebPizza is a completely client-side application. This means:

  • ❌ We do NOT collect any personal information
  • ❌ We do NOT store your documents on our servers
  • ❌ We do NOT track your browsing activity
  • ❌ We do NOT use cookies for tracking
  • ❌ We do NOT send your data to third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT have access to your uploaded PDFs or questions

3. How WebPizza Works

All processing happens entirely in your browser using:

  • WebGPU/WebAssembly: For running AI models locally on your device
  • IndexedDB: For storing document embeddings locally in your browser
  • Local Storage: For caching AI models to improve performance

Your documents and conversations never leave your device. Everything is processed locally.

4. Local Browser Storage

WebPizza uses browser storage technologies to function:

  • IndexedDB: Stores document chunks and embeddings locally
  • Cache API/IndexedDB Cache: Stores AI models for faster loading

You have full control: You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings or by clearing browser cache.

5. Third-Party AI Models

AI models are downloaded from:

  • Hugging Face CDN: For LLM models (WebLLM/WeInfer)
  • CDN providers: For embedding models and libraries

These downloads are one-time and cached locally. After the initial download, no further communication with these services occurs during your usage of the application.

6. Hosting

This application is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, access times) for infrastructure purposes. Please refer to Vercel's Privacy Policy for more information.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data for us to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Export

All your data is stored locally on your device and is under your complete control.

8. Security

Because all processing happens client-side:

  • Your documents never transit over the network (except for the initial page load)
  • No server-side vulnerabilities can expose your data
  • Your data security depends on your device and browser security

9. Children's Privacy

Our service does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
